We are Survivors

For the past 2 years since the pandemic, we all have experienced the pains of adjusting to working from home. Making some of us survivors. We also enjoyed all of the perks that go along with it. As a survivor and a keen observer, I compiled all of the practical tips I’ve learned to endure the work-from-home game.

For those undergoing the battle and fighting to win the war, I know that you are all natural survivors. But in case you need help to know your focus and identify pitfalls in the work from home setup faster than anybody else, you might benefit from our book, “Survival Guide to Work From Home.”

About the Author

Noel “NOIEE” Peralta Jr. is the CEO of One Knowledge Consulting, LLC. He advocates for the survival of his fellow workers who seek to earn a decent living by working from home amid the ongoing pandemic.

Peralta is a graduate of Bachelor of Science in Information Technology at the Mapua Institute in the Philippines. One Knowledge Consulting, LLC is based in Florida and specializes in assisting businesses, big or small, with marketing boost through digital platforms. Most of the OK staff are working from home.

About the Book

Survivors Series

Forbes, BBC, and NBC have all predicted working from home as a long-term work setup. The absence of a perfect cure to Covid-19 infection and its mutations made it difficult for workers to return to their offices.

The work-from-home setup has saved several businesses that needed to operate amid the pandemic since 2020. But the changes and adjustments can be overwhelming to some of us. Can we survive this?

The "Survival Guide to Work From Home" contains vital information about the new work setup, shared within the perspective of a soldier who set out to survive the war.

The book is a compilation of all of the practical tips that can help workers to endure the work-from-home game.

The book also features real-life stories of work from home workers who've "survived," by successfully adjusting to their new setup and preventing the pitfalls. It also contains enjoyable and endearing illustrations.
